Microbiology Glossary

MIC (Minimum Inhibitory Concentration): The lowest concentration of an antibiotic that prevents visible bacterial growth.
Horizontal Gene Transfer: The movement of genetic material between bacteria through conjugation, transformation, or transduction.
CFU (Colony Forming Units): A unit used to estimate the number of viable bacteria in a sample.
Biofilm: A structured community of bacteria enclosed in a self-produced polymeric matrix.
